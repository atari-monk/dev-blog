# Content Strategy

## Prepared Content Benefits

- **Higher Engagement** – Show finished products or key milestones
- **More Entertaining** – Edit for pacing, highlight features, or add demos
- **Less Pressure** – Refine explanations offline
- **Better Retention** – Fast-paced, visually focused content keeps viewers

## Engagement Techniques

- **Show & Tell** – Demo builds, explain challenges, walk through code
- **Interactive Elements** – Solicit feedback or feature ideas from viewers
- **Time-Lapse Coding** – Speed up recordings with post-commentary
- **Tutorial Streams** – Teach recently mastered concepts

## Hybrid Approach

- **Occasional Live Coding** – Focus on small features or bug fixes
- **Polished Streams** – Prioritize demos, Q&As, or project reviews

## Stream Formats

- *"Built X in Y days—here’s how"* (Demo + code walkthrough)
- *"Fixing critical bugs in real-time"* (Problem-solving session)
- *"Framework A vs B for Project Y"* (Technical comparison)
